TWICE will release their 2nd full-length album – “Eyes Wide Open” - on October 26th

It is the second full-length album of TWICE after releasing “Twicetagram”, the first full-length album, on Oct 30, 2017. It reveals the charm of TWICE more boldly.

TWICE has challenged various genres of music covering dance-pop (overflowing with grooves), city pop, R&B, and Latin pop through “Eyes Wide Open”.

While they sang, the feeling of yearning for love grew more as they fall deeper in love in “More & More”, the previous song, which they put newly recognized feelings in the music this time.

The title track, “I Can’t Stop Me”, sings of a conflicting heart on the edge of good and evil and an uncontrollable mind. It falls in the synthwave genre, which is the mix of European electronic sound and American 80s synth sound and features an addictive melody.

J.Y. Park, the head of JYP Entertainment, was in charge of writing the title track again, after writing many hits of TWICE including “Signal”, “What is Love?”, “Feel Special”, and “More & More”.

The lyricist Eunji Shim, who has written numerous hit songs, has joined the team as well. They, solid strings of box office hits, joined forces to aim for “14 consecutive hits”

Moreover, it was composed by Melanie Joy Fontana, who is a global hitmaker and worked with top artists such as BTS and Halsey, and Michel ‘Lindgren’ Schulz, a famous producer.

Twice presents a more powerful and splendid performance in the new song, “I Can’t Stop Me”. The choreography was completed by the cooperation of Kiel Tutin, who collaborated with Twice for “More & More”, and Jonte Moaning, Beyonce's choreographer.

The story of its music video is connected to that of More & More’s music video. It boasts a wealth of attractions with fantastic backgrounds such as gorges and train stations. It is also worth mentioning the sensational and colorful graphic effects, including the nine members appearing in the flowers.

The new album “Eyes Wide Open” contains 13 songs: “I Can’t Stop Me”, “Hell in Heaven”, “Up No More”, “Do What We Like”, “Bring it Back”, “Believer”, “Queen”, “Go Hard”, “Shot Clock”, “Handle It”, “Depend on You”, “Say Something”, and “Behind the Mask”.

It has been drawing a lot of attention owing to the participation of world-renowned artists, such as J.Y. Park, Eunji Shim, Dua Lipa, Kenzie. Brilliant artists, including Kenzi, who is called a “legendary song maker” among K-pop fans, LDN Noise, a British producing team, Dua Lipa, a famous British artist with world-class fandom, MNEK, who participated in producing “More & More”, collapsedone who participated in “Knock Knock” of TWICE and “You Were Beautiful” of DAY6, earattack who worked with successful K-pop artists such as GOT7 and ITZY, and Heize, who is a singer-songwriter with unrivaled sensibility, turned out for making the second full-length album of TWICE.
