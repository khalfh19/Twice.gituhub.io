feel special

TWICE announces the 8th Mini Album with the title song 'Feel Special'!

  • "Have you ever felt like you are a “special being”?”
  • A gift-like story to everyone who gave 'a brilliant moment' to the nine members
  • “I, who used to be nothing, have become a special being thanks to you.”

  • - The new album “Feel Special” captures the heart-throbbing moments shining each other!
  • - Title song 'Feel Special', the combination of “J.Y. Park X collapsedone”! Aim for “12 consecutive hits”
  • - High-quality album with the participation of top composers including TWICE! "Parade of masterpieces"

TWICE - “the Best K-Pop Girl Group” – is back with the 8th mini album, “Feel Special”.

TWICE has maintained its top position since its debut in 2015. TWICE has succeeded in all songs from debut song “OOH-AHH하게”, to all following songs “CHEER UP”, “TT”, “KNOCK KNOCK”, “SIGNAL”, ”LIKEY”, “Heart Shaker”, ”What is Love?”, ”Dance The Night Away”, “YES or YES”, and “FANCY”. TWICE now aim for’12 consecutive hits’ with “Feel Special” that grafts new messages and lyricism.

“Feel Special”, the new album, includes songs: “Feel Special” (the same name with the album title), “RAINBOW”, “GET LOUD”, “TRICK IT”, “LOVE FOOLISH”, “21:29”, and Korean version “BREAKTHROUGH”, which was in the Japan 5th Single.

“Feel Special”, the title song, describes the heart-throbbing moment given by loved ones when we are walking through a heart’s tunnel as dark as the lightless night. It is even more special that the song was inspired by the conversation between J.Y. Park, chief producer of JYP Entertainment, and TWICE members. The feelings that TWICE have felt in the past 4 years after their debut with overcoming difficult moments were the starting point of this song. J.Y. Park wrote this song by reflecting “the wish to get up again upon the support of someone’s warm words when I am afraid and want to collapse.” TWICE also sang it with the hope that it would encourage everyone. Collapsedone, who composed “KNOCK KNOCK” and arranged “What is Love?” with J.Y. Park, participated in the arrangement of this song. “Feel Special” is another masterpiece created upon the collaboration of “J.Y. Park X TWICE X collapsedone”.

The music video of “Feel Special” expresses the “special moment”, the main theme of the song, with light and colorful objects to provide a unique visual pleasure. It is evaluated that it contains not only TWICE's unique dynamic energy but also trendy charm. The bright smile of looking at each other, who have made them feel special, maximizes the song's message and lyrical atmosphere.

The extravaganza lineup showed in the album credit also attracts all listeners’ attention: Melanie Fontana, the creator of BTS’s “Boy With Luv”, JQ who wrote EXO’s “Tempo”, Ryan S. Jhun, who participate in Red Velvet’s “Dumb Dumb” and EXO’s “LOVE ME RIGHT”, and Sim Eun Jee who worked with TWICE for “YES or YES” and “KNOCK”. The impressive artists have increased the completeness of the album.

TWICE, who has been working on songs since the 2nd Mini Album, has actively participated in the production of this new album and showed off musical growth. NAYEON wrote the lyrics of “RAINBOW” alone and JIHYO participated in “GET LOUD” to deliver a strong message following the last album “FANCY YOU”. DAHYUN challenged to write the lyrics for the first time and melted her unique emotion into “TRICK IT”. MOMO added a touch to “LOVE FOOLISH” by working with delicate and sensitive artist Sim Eun Jee.
