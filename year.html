TWICE Releases The 3rd Special Album ‘The year of “YES”’ and The Title song ‘The Best Thing I Ever Did’ on December 12th

  • - The Best Thing TWICE Ever Did in 2018? “The Most Precious Memory Is Meeting People Who We Love.”
  • - “TWICE’s the First R&B Challenge”… The Title Song ‘The Best Thing I Ever Did’, Created with The ‘Blank Filling’ Composition Method of JYP Entertainment J.Y. Park “The Asiansoul”
  • - The New Gift-Like Album that TWICE Presents to the Fans, ‘The year of “YES”’ Contains a Total of 9 Tracks! 'The Grand Finale of 2018’

TWICE releases The 3rd Special Album ‘The year of “YES”’ and its title Song ‘The Best Thing I Ever Did’ on December 12th.

Starting with The 5th Mini Album ‘What is Love? on April, 2018, TWICE released The 2nd Special Album ‘Summer Nights’ on July, and The 6th Mini Album ‘YES or YES’ on November. And now TWICE announces The 3rd Special Album ‘The year of “YES”’ on December to show their hearts for the fans who showed everlasting love throughout the year.

The title song of The 3rd Special Album ‘The year of “YES”’, ‘The Best Thing I Ever Did’ is a combination of Alternative R&B genre and TWICE’s unique pop sound, whose strong groove stands out. The lyrics contain the message that ‘taking a moment to review the year in December, the most precious memory is meeting people who we love.’

TWICE presents new charms by challenging R&B song for the first time with the new song ‘The Best Thing I Ever Did’.

Especially, ‘The Best Thing I Ever Did’ attracted the attention even before the official release as J.Y. Park “The Asiansoul”, the leader of JYP Entertainment, and Park Ji Min wrote and composed. J.Y. Park “The Asiansoul” created ‘The Best Thing I Ever Did’ with a new composition method called ‘Blank Filling’. Once J.Y. Park “The Asiansoul” wrote and composed the chorus part, he shared the rest part of the song only with accompaniment to his colleague composers to fill in the blank. It is the first time that J.Y. Park “The Asiansoul” completed the song writing with this ‘Blank Filling’ method, and he attempted such challenge in order to express various musical characteristics by cooperating with the colleague composers. As a result, the chorus of ‘The Best Thing I Ever Did’ was made by J.Y. Park “The Asiansoul”, while the verse and the bridge were made by the 6 composers together, including the artist Park Ji Min.

Moreover, The 3rd Special Album contains the Korean Version of The 1st J-Pop Album ‘BDZ’ track ‘Be as ONE’. ‘Be as ONE (Korean Ver.)’ is the first Ballad genre presented by TWICE, and it sings about ‘the true heart to be as one by depending on each other.’ The harmony of the calm and refined piano play and the sensuous string is impressive. The arrangement of the instruments that opens the climax along with the harmony and ballad vocal of TWICE members evoke emotion.

In addition to the same name title song of The 6th Mini Album ‘YES or YES’, TWICE’s new album contains a total of 9 tracks including 'SAY YOU LOVE ME', 'LALALA', 'YOUNG & WILD', 'SUNSET', 'AFTER MOON', and 'BDZ (Korean Ver.),’ increasing the value of possession.

TWICE achieved the records of 1 million sales with the 3 albums released earlier this year. Furthermore, the title song of each album, “What is Love?”, “Dance The Night Away”, and “YES or YES” all ranked on the top of the online real-time, daily, and weekly charts as well as Gaon Chart.
