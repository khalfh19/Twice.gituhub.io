cry for me

TWICE, a “representative K-pop girl group”, released “CRY FOR ME”, a digital single, on December 18th!

  • - “CRY FOR ME”, which made global fans excited, now available after the release of “2020 MAMA”!
  • - Written by Jin-young Park, a representative producer of JYP, & Heize, a singer-songwriter! Singing the confused emotions of entangled love and hate!
  • - A powerful magic of love shown by TWICE! Inescapable black-hole-like emotion!

TWICE will release its digital single, “CRY FOR ME”, on December 18th!

TWICE unveiled CRY FOR ME, an unreleased song, at the “2020 Mnet Asian Music Awards”, aired on December 6th, for the first time

On the stage, the members enchanted the audience at once owing to their fascinating facial expressions and gorgeously overwhelming performances.

The video of the performance exceeded 9 million views in less than a week after being posted on “Mnet K-POP”, the Mnet's official YouTube channel''.

“CRY FOR ME” sings about the confusion in which love and hate are entangled, and you can feel the even deeper and stronger charm of TWICE.

The lyrics are written by Jin-young Park, JYP Entertainment's head and a representative K-pop producer, and Heize, a popular singer-songwriter. It depicts the moment when the extremes of both emotions coexist.

Moreover, it is composed by Ryan Tedder, a member of the famous emo rock band “OneRepublic”, Melanie Joy Fontana, a global hit song maker, and Michelle 'Lindgren' Schulz, a renowned producer.

TWICE will reveal a black-hole-like charm with this new song “CRY FOR ME” that has captured the public’s attention before its official release.
